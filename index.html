Tom and Katy's Wedding

Our Story

Our wedding story starts like anyone else's, I got down on one knee, shook my then boyfriend (now fiance!!!) until he woke up from his sleep and asked him to marry me. He answered "What time is it? No, I absolutely will not marry you. Get back in bed, it's 4 in the morning."
Obviously I was thrilled and started planning straight away. However, I'm not sure about some of the wedding stuff and Tom wont help me with it, saying stuff like "We're not engaged, stop telling people that. You told my mam, what the fuck?" and also "I'm seriously concerned right now.". So I've decided to get you, the visitor to this website, to help me plan it. I hope that you are as excited as I am.
